ABOUT US

At Cunto Wacane, we believe that dining is more than just eating; it's an experience that should engage all your senses. Nestled in the heart of hargeisa, we are a culinary haven where passion meets innovation, and every meal tells a story.

Founded in 2017, Cunto Wacane was born out of a shared love for exceptional food and warm hospitality. The founders, Madr abdi envisioned a place where people could come together to savor delicious dishes crafted with care and creativity.

We invite you to join us at Cunto Wacane and indulge in a culinary adventure that transcends the ordinary. From the first bite to the last sip, we aim to make every moment memorable. Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to serving you soon.
